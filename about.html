About the Managing Partner

Seongbin Lee the Managing Partner and Founder of NAVITAS, a design and technology agency. He is currently a student at YCIS in Shanghai, China. He founded NAVITAS so that he could contribute to the society with his passion for technology and design. His primary strengths are in website development and application prototyping. He is currently looking for teammates to create more awesome projects.

More intriguing information

One of the major projects of NAVITAS in the past include the creation of the website for GOEL. GOEL is a social enterprise that aims to increase the digital literacy of the youth in Zimbabwe, and has offices in Zimbabwe and Seoul. He keenly communicated with the Managing Partner of GOEL to make sure to create a high quality website. Because GOEL was satisfied with the work of NAVITAS, Seongbin was introduced to Star Alliance Africa, a media and culture company based in South Africa that incubates musical talent.
