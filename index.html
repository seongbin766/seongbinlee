What I do

These are short descriptions about the books I have made.

Jacky

This book is all about a monster named Jacky who was formed by a Nuke Bomb during the Korean War. He is a threat to mankind, and mankind has no chance to stop him: only if you are Zane.

Mystery of the missing iPad

This short story is about an iPad which is the spirit of a mansion that got stolen by a bunch of burglars who lived in an outpost near the mansion.

Jacky Raids Again

After Jacky and Zane both died, Gilly said that a second Jacky may appear. And it did. This book is all about the second Jacky and a new monster named Julierus decimating Shanghai.

Kaandan

This book is all about a winged monster who was destroying things in his path, and eating insects like the Maddiebugs.

Jacky: King of the Reptiles!

This book is simply the American version of the original Jacky. No description here. #Appology.

Alien Origins

The story is about an evil alien gang called the Nuke Puppies. They have a war with Aiden and his immense army of henchmen.
